About me

Bachelor in Communication

"The more human a brand, the deeper it connects."

I'm a native Spanish copywriter with experience in journalism, advertising, and digital content creation. I write copy, scripts, captions, and campaigns entirely in Spanish — for brands targeting Latin American and Spanish-speaking audiences. I've worked across 4 countries: Ecuador, Colombia, Peru, and the Dominican Republic.

I love telling stories that hook people, riding trends, and giving authentic voice to brands. I believe content isn't just information — it's emotion, connection, and community.

My background in media — including Ecuavisa and Revista Vistazo — gave me the journalistic rigor I now apply to the world of digital marketing.
